What to Look For in an AC Under ₹30,000?

Before buying the best AC under 30000, it helps to understand which specs actually matter for Indian homes and which are mostly marketing.

Capacity is the most critical decision. As a general rule, 1 ton is suitable for rooms up to 110 to 120 square feet. 1.5 ton covers 120 to 150 square feet. Undersizing means the compressor runs at full load constantly and your room never fully cools in peak summer. Oversizing causes short cycling — the unit reaches target temperature too quickly, shuts off, and restarts repeatedly, which wastes electricity and wears out the compressor faster.

Always choose copper coils over aluminium. Every AC on this list uses copper, which is the right standard. Copper coils are more durable, easier to repair, and hold up better in Indian humidity and pollution levels. Aluminium coils are cheaper to manufacture but significantly harder and more expensive to repair when they develop leaks.

Inverter technology is non-negotiable at this budget. All five ACs here use inverter compressors, which vary their speed based on cooling demand rather than switching on and off at full power. This saves 30 to 50% on electricity compared to fixed-speed compressors and reduces wear on the unit over time. There is no reason to consider a non-inverter AC in 2026.

Understand the 2026 BEE star rating change. BEE updated its ISEER standards in 2026, raising the minimum threshold for each star band. This means a 2024 5-star AC may now carry a 3-star label under the new system — not because it got worse, but because the standard got stricter. Always check the ISEER number on the BEE label rather than relying on star count alone. An ISEER above 4.0 is solid at this price range.

After-sales service matters more than any feature. An AC requires installation, annual maintenance, and occasional gas top-ups. Blue Star, Whirlpool, and Carrier have the most reliable service networks in India. Cruise is newer and still building coverage. In tier-2 or tier-3 cities, prioritise a brand whose service centre you can actually locate before buying.

R-32 refrigerant is the right choice. All five models here use R-32, which has 68% lower global warming potential than the older R-410A and is more efficient at heat transfer. It is the current standard for new AC installations in India and is worth confirming before buying any model not on this list.
